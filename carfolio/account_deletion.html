Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Carfolio account and all associated data, you can do so easily.

Warning: Deleting your account is irreversible. All your vehicles, logs, expenses, and photos will be permanently removed from our servers.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the app. This will immediately remove your data from our servers.

  1. Open the Carfolio App.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap on Account (top card with your profile).
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Type DELETE to confirm the action.

Method 2: Email Request

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, you may request account deletion via email. Please allow up to 30 days for processing.

Send an email to support@carfolio.app with the following details:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Email Body: Please include the email address associated with your Carfolio account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials.
  • All vehicle data (VIN, make, model, etc.).
  • All service logs, fuel logs, and expense records.
  • All uploaded photos and documents.

Note: We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention), as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
