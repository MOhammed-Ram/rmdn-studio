Request Account Deletion

Carfolio Application

We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete your details, you can do so easily. Note that this action is irreversible.

Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all vehicles, logs, expenses, and photos from our servers.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the app:

  1. Open the Carfolio App.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap on Account (top profile card).
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Type DELETE to confirm.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion via email (allow up to 30 days for processing).

Send an email to support@carfolio.app with:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: The email address associated with your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile & authentication credentials.
  • All vehicle data (VIN, details).
  • All service, fuel, and expense logs.
  • Uploaded receipt photos and documents.
