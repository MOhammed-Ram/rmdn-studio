Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Carfolio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Vehicle Data: Make, model, year, license plate, VIN, and other vehicle details.
  • Service & Fuel Logs: Odometer readings, fuel costs, service records, and expenses.
  • Documents & Images: Photos of receipts, licenses, and other documents you upload.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (for analytics and debugging).
  • Usage Data: Interactions with the App, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Location Data: If you grant permission, we may collect your coarse or precise location to help you find nearby fuel stations or log fill-up locations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (tracking vehicles, expenses, and reminders).
  • Sync your data across devices (if Cloud Sync is enabled).
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions (via RevenueCat).
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob).
  • Improve the App through analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party service providers to facilitate App functionality:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, cloud database (Firestore), and secure storage.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to provide personalized ads.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. Transaction data is processed securely; we do not store your credit card information.
  • Google ML Kit: Used for on-device text recognition (OCR) specifically for scanning receipts and VINs. Processing happens locally on your device.

4. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera / Photo Gallery: To scan VINs, capture receipts, and upload vehicle photos.
  • Location: To assist in logging fuel station locations.
  • Biometrics (FaceID/TouchID): To secure access to the App. Biometric data is processed locally by the operating system and is never stored or transmitted by us.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time via the specific "Delete Account" option in the Settings menu.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Affiliate Disclosure

Carfolio participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through affiliate links within the App.
