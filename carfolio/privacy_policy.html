Privacy Policy

Carfolio Application

Last updated: January 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RMDN STUDIOS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, Carfolio (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Vehicle Data: Make, model, year, license plate, VIN, and other vehicle details.
  • Logs & Expenses: Odometer readings, fuel costs, service records, and other maintenance data.
  • Documents: Photos of receipts, licenses, and insurance cards you upload.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers (for analytics/debugging).
  • Usage Data: Crash logs, performance metrics, and app interaction data.
  • Location Data: (Optional) Coarse or precise location to help find fuel stations or log fill-up locations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features (tracking maintenance, calculating fuel efficiency).
  • To sync data across your devices via secure cloud storage.
  • To process subscriptions and payments (via RevenueCat/App Stores).
  • To improve app stability and performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to support our App:

  • Google Firebase: Secure backend for authentication, database (Firestore), and storage.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription, purchase validation, and entitlement management.
  • Google AdMob: Displaying advertisements (if applicable).
  • Google ML Kit: On-device machine learning for text recognition (VIN/Receipt scanning).

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data as long as your account is active. You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Account: You can permanently delete your account and all cloud data instantly via the Settings > Account > Delete Account option in the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Use of the App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
