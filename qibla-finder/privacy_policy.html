Privacy Policy

Qibla Finder by RMDN STUDIOS

Last updated: December 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RMDN STUDIOS ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application, Qibla Finder (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

To fulfill the core functionality of the App (calculating the Qibla direction and accurate prayer times), we require access to your device's precise location (GPS).

  • Purpose: To calculate the bearing from your current coordinates to the Kaaba in Mecca and to determine local prayer times based on the sun's position.
  • Storage: This location data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not transmit your location history to our servers for storage or tracking purposes.

Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) for analytics and troubleshooting.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to improve our App, process subscriptions, and serve advertisements. These services may collect and process information subject to their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob

3. Subscription and Payments

Payments for premium features are processed directly by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not view or store your credit card or financial information.

4. Your Choices

  • Location: You can revoke location permissions at any time via your device settings. However, doing so will disable the Qibla compass and automatic prayer time features.
  • Tracking: You may opt-out of personalized advertising and tracking via your device's privacy settings (e.g., "Ask App Not to Track" on iOS or "Opt-out of Ads Personalization" on Android).
