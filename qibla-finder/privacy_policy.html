Privacy Policy

Qibla Finder Application

Last updated: January 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RMDN STUDIOS ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application, Qibla Finder (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Location Data (GPS)

To fulfill the core functionality of the App (calculating Qibla direction and accurate prayer times), we require access to your device's precise location.

  • Purpose: To calculate the bearing to the Kaaba and determine local prayer times based on the sun's position.
  • Storage: Location data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not transmit your location history to our servers for storage or tracking.

Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, including hardware model, OS version, and unique identifiers (for analytics and debugging).

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to improve our App:

  • Google AdMob: May use device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase validation. Anonymous purchase data is shared to unlock premium features.

3. Subscription and Payments

Payments for premium features are processed directly by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not access or store your financial information.

4. Your Choices

  • Location: You can revoke location permissions in device settings. Note that this will disable the compass and prayer time features.
  • Tracking: You may opt-out of personalized ads via device settings (e.g., "Ask App Not to Track").
